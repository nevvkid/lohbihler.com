About

Johannes Elias Lohbihler is a seasoned product designer / creative technologist.
Amongst other ventures, he founded dadamachines – a studio for multisensory experiences & open-source music technology.
Recently he did a stint at Supernova and helped KORG – setting up shop in Berlin.
His expertise is ranging from crafts to creating digital and analog experiences, products, or services for large & small clients.
